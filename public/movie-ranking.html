Movie Review by Avik

Top 5 Movies of All Time

1. Kantara

The best movie there is in terms of
1. Real spirituality
2. Closeness to Bharatiya Culture
3. Grace of the Gods

2. HanuMan

Even though it mixes truth with superheroism
1. Still makes us think of Lord Hanuman
2. Still based in Bharatiya culture
3. Has the Hanuman chalisa and a great animation of Lord Hanuman

One downside is the disrespect of saints. Which is quite a substantial downside.
Another is disrespect of Anajanadri traditions and their traditional wrestlers

3. The Pursuit of Happyness

One of the most motivational films pertaining to work
1. Shows real world challenges and how to overcome them
2. Is a biography, hence is accurate
3. Shows corporate life, and how we must be willing to work every living moment.

4. Interstellar

Is a great sci-fi film with real physics in significant portions
1. Black hole is made with consultation of physicists
2. Is very inspirational to push for technological growth of humanity
3. Has real aerospace physics when it come to rocketry (except last part)

5. RRR

Has a great message and increases our natonal pride
1. Story talks about freedom struggle
2. Promotes Hindutva
3. Deeply imbibes sentiment of national service
